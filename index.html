Chocolate Cake

Soft • Creamy • Delicious

⏱ Prep Time

45 Minutes

🍽 Servings

4 People

⏳ Cooking Timer

45:00

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter for greasing

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Beat eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add milk and vanilla essence.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Pour batter into greased tray.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Cool and serve the chocolate cake.
